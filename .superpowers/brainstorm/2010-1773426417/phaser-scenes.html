How should scene transitions work?

A

Scene Switch with Fade (Recommended)

Use Phaser's built-in scene.start('SettlementScene') with a camera fade effect. The previous scene is stopped (not destroyed). Returning to the regional map restarts it. Simple, standard Phaser pattern.

Flow: RegionMap → fade out → SettlementScene loads → fade in. Press M → fade out → RegionMap restarts → fade in.

B

Parallel Scenes (Both Alive)

Both RegionMap and Settlement scenes run simultaneously. One is active + visible, the other is paused + hidden. Toggle between them. Preserves state in both directions — your position on the regional map is remembered.

Flow: RegionMap (active) → scene.sleep('RegionMapScene') + scene.wake('SettlementScene'). Press M → reverse.
